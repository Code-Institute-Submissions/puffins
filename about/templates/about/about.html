About us

We at Puffins are dedicated to provide beautiful, uniting and sustainable products to individuals, love birds and families.

Our Vision

At Puffins we believe that everything has a purpose. With our method to only produce whats demanded by our customers and delivering longlasting quality products we want to counter the biggest problems of the western society. Our products are locally produced and we only use materials where we know the origin, to guarantee 100% ethical and sustainable products.

Our Story

We are a small family that shares the desire to spend as much time outside as possible. Our interests range from bird watching, wave and kite surfing, fishing, hiking, running, traveling, camping and climbing to cooking, making smores over a campfire, studying sunsets and much much more. With the love for the outdoors and an active lifestyle we want to provide beautiful and sustainable products to like-minded awesome people and build a caring, sustainable and inspiring community.
